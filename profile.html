Welcome to Rising-Group

Rising Group started with 120 machines in 1997 in a rented building and now one of the leading apparel, yarn, knit fabrics manufacturer located located at its own two ten and seven storied buildings at Mirpur having a total floor space of 124,000 Sq. Ft. In addition another six storied building is located at Ashulia, Dhaka, each floor measuring 13,500 Sq. Ft. & total of 81,000 Sq. Ft. and designed with Sewing, Cutting, Finishing and Packing.

Mission

In quest of professionalism and perfection characterized by a relentless pursuit for innovations and a focus towards nurturing lasting customer relationship.
Vision

Groups investment will continuously be directed at quality manpower, cutting edge information technology, product refinement and survive through the changes as the present century demands. Background

A small manufacturing & exporting of apparels unit named Rising Fashions Ltd was established in 1997 with 120-machines only. Meeting customer's demand for manufacturing woven apparels and most often their expectations were not difficult. Things started to change dramatically over the last three years. Group passionately committed to provide quality services to the enhanced needs to our customers.
