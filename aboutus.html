COMPRESSED GROUP

pays significant contribution in Bangladesh apparel exports, which has Vertically integrated manufacturing units of textile products including a large Spinning Mill equipped with 30,240 Spindles ring and 720 Spindles rotor machines which is under operation since 2005. In the year 2008, we start our journey towards the Knit Fabric Manufacturing & Finishing with capacity of 15 – 16 ton fabric/day for different types of Knit Fabrics.

A progressive professional management team and a good employee friendly program are set-up to run marketing, sourcing, commercial and production. All manufacturing units are well lighted and ventilated with industrial safety measures as described by the local regulatory laws and buyers Code of Conduct. About Us

Corporate Group started with 120 machines in 1997 in a rented building and now one of the leading apparel, yarn, knit fabrics manufacturer located located at its own two ten and seven storied buildings at Mirpur having a total floor space of 124,000 Sq. Ft. In addition another six storied building is located at Ashulia, Dhaka each floor measuring 13,500 Sq.Ft. & total of 81,000 Sq.Ft. and designed with Sewing, Cutting, Finishing and Packing.
