PRACTICE & CLIMATES

RISING GROUP with its progressive business growth, believes and practices corporate culture an attractive, congenial and dynamic working environment based on efficiency and equity. RISING GROUP believes in company growth by increasing efficiency level of employees & workers with constant opportunities for development and diversified business activities by providing excellent support to enhance knowledge, ability and skill. The relationship of employee-employer is cordial supportive to each other

Employee Friendly Environment
  • Equal opportunity for all in spite of gender, cast, creed, religion and political belief in terms of appointment, promotion, increments and benifit as per Regulatory Laws.
  • Full time Medical Officer for free consultation and limited medicinal support in addition to fixed medical allowances as per regulatory laws.
  • Referral services arranged in ahospital at a reduced cost those having Health Insurances with a very nominal premium (US$ 1) per year
  • Advocacy on family welfare, food & nutrition and personal hygiene given to workers 4-days a week by Para-medics from internationally recognized non-governmental organization Gono Shasta Kandra !GSK) from.
  • Medical bills paid in full in case of accidents & hospitalization
  • Workers & employees enjoy all mandatory leaves as per local law
  • Femele workers/employees enjoy Maternity Leave of 16 weeks with full pay
  • Two Festival bonuses given to workers/employees these who completed one year
  • Attendance bonus and incentive bonus given to workers
  • Day Care facility arranged for worker's children under 5-years of age
Employee Communication
  • Regular briefings are given to workers on local laws & regulations their rights & entitlements by organizing meetings and periodic announcements using central address system.
  • A Booklet explaining local laws & regulations are given to all workers at the time of recruitment and orientation organized.
  • All factories ensure health & safety measures as per local regulatory laws and regular monitoring performed.

Ft. In addition another six storied building is located at Ashulia, Dhaka each floor measuring 13,500 Sq.Ft. & total of 81,000 Sq.Ft. and designed with Sewing, Cutting, Finishing and Packing.
